History of TechCon

TechCon started in 2010 with the mission to bring together industry leaders, innovators, and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, TechCon has evolved into one of the most highly anticipated technology conferences in the world. The event focuses on cutting-edge topics and fosters collaboration between the brightest minds in the tech community.

Mission of TechCon 2024

Our mission is to inspire and empower professionals, startups, and technology enthusiasts to innovate and collaborate on the future of technology. TechCon 2024 will focus on the transformative power of artificial intelligence, sustainability in tech, and the future of work.
